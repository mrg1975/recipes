Ingredients:

1 Pepper diced
1 Can of chick peas
1/2 Can of lentils
1 Handful of green beans, chopped
1 Onion chopped
1 Clove of garlic crushed
1 Tin chopped tomatoes
2 table spoons of medium curry powder

Fry the onions until soft, then add the garlic, curry powder and pepper.
After 2 more minutes add the tomatoes, beans , chickpeas, and lentils.
Cover and simmer for 30 minutes.

Serve with rice.
